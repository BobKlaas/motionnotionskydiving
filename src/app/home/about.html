The Notion of Motion

Motion Notion was created by Bob Klaas and Nadav Schwartz in 2016. Based out of Skydive Elsinore, they wanted to start a movement geared towards dynamic freeflying. This movement was fueled by their love for motion and individual expression. As the idea evolved, the brand Motion Notion was born.

Motion Notion in its direct definition translates to "the idea of motion". However, it embodies something far greater. It embodies a lifestyle that encourages motion, taking chances, flying your own line, living your passion, and sharing that passion with others. What started out as an idea, is now real. We love skydiving, expression, life, and we welcome you to join us on this journey. Let's keep things in motion.

Our Vision

To empower personal expression while pushing the progression of dynamic freeflying.

Our Mission

To inspire, up-lift, and share the language of dynamic motion throughout the skydiving community.
