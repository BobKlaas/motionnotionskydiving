The Notion of Motion

As we move through life, we experience fear, stress, anxiety, hardship, and uncertainty. These feelings move in and out of our experience. They eventually give way to moments of strength, happiness, laughter, and love. Collectively, they bring us together and give us a true sense of our presence in this space.

We see motion in this same light. When we are able to unplug from the ordinary, we are able to experience real, organic, flight. By letting go, we become free to express ourselves. It’s during these moments that we will discover our true expression and style.

We believe that every skydive is a choice. A choice to transcend fear. A choice to be present. A choice to let go of the ordinary. However, with every choice, we are also gifted an opportunity. An opportunity to smile. An opportunity to feel connected. An opportunity to learn about who we really are.

Motion Notion is about sharing our essence with each other. It’s about sharing the space we occupy together; in the sky and on the ground. We welcome you to join us in this space.

Thank you for sharing your essence with us. We are humbled. Live MoNo.

Our Mission

To share the language of organic motion while encouraging personal expression and authenticity.

Our Vision

To create a lifestyle that embodies organic flight, individual expression, and the essence of skydiving.
