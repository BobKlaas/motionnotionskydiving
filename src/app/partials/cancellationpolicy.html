Cancellation Policy
  • Attendees who cancel 48 hours or more prior to the event will be eligible for a full refund minus a $5.00 processing fee.
  • Attendees who cancel 24 hours or more prior to the event will be eligible for a 50% refund.
  • Attendees who cancel less than 24 hours before the event will not be eligible for a refund.
  • Refunds will be issued via PayPal or credited back to the card used at time of payment.
Weather Policy
  • If an event gets completely weathered out and no skydives are made, a full refund minus a $5 processing fee will be offered to all attendees.
  • If at least 50% of the planned jumps are made, attendees will not be eligible for a refund.
